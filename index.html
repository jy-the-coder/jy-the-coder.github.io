Restaurant Analysis Platform Guide
1. Select Region
Choose the region you want to analyze from the 'Target Region' dropdown at the top. Once you select a region, the overall market ecosystem information for that area will be displayed.
2. Choose Cuisine Type
After selecting a region, choose your cuisine of interest from the 'Cuisine Focus' dropdown. Detailed analysis and competitive information for the selected cuisine will appear.
3. Dashboard Interpretation
Market Ecosystem Health: Shows diversity, saturation, opportunity scores, etc. for the region.
Cuisine Market Analysis: Displays detailed data and trends for the selected cuisine.
Competitive Intelligence: Analyzes major competitors and their strengths.
Market Opportunities: Presents business opportunities discovered in the current market.
4. Strategic Insights Utilization
Check the 'Strategic Business Insights' section at the bottom for specific business recommendations and strategies for your selected region and cuisine. Use this information to make data-driven decisions.
Chart Tips: Chart Interpretation Tips
Each chart includes a legend to help you easily understand the data. Hover over charts to see detailed information and understand the meaning of each element.
